========================================
CCNA Lab #12 – EtherChannel Configuration
========================================

VLAN Structure
--------------
VLAN 10 – HR
VLAN 20 – SALES
VLAN 30 – IT


========================================
CORE-1 Configuration
========================================

enable
configure terminal

vlan 10
 name HR
vlan 20
 name SALES
vlan 30
 name IT

interface range fa0/1-4
 switchport mode trunk
 channel-group 1 mode active

interface port-channel 1
 switchport mode trunk

end
